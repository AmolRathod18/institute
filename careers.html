Program Overview

Our internship program is designed to give aspiring developers and testers real-world experience working on actual client projects. You'll be paired with experienced mentors who will guide your learning and professional development.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on experience with real projects
  • Mentorship from senior developers
  • Training in modern web technologies
  • Agile development methodology exposure
  • Professional networking opportunities
  • Certificate upon successful completion
  • Potential for full-time employment

Available Tracks

Frontend Development

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React

Software Testing

Manual & Automated QA

UI/UX Design

User Research, Prototyping

Full Stack Development

Frontend + Backend Integration

Requirements

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field
  • Basic understanding of web technologies
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt
  • Availability for 3-6 month commitment
